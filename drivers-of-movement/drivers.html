Drivers of Movement

This proposed factor model containing sixteen push and pull factors, including national and regional macro-level factors (economic, political, environmental), intervening meso-level factors (relief, weather, infrastructure), and individual household micro-level factors (household demographics, capital). Each factor can be operationalized as a set of variables generated from different data sources. For example, a traditional environment variable may be amount of precipitation, while weather buzz is an example of an environment big data variable.
